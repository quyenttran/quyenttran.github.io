The Healing Power of Yoga

Many people view yoga as a form of exercise, but traditionally, it is used as a moving meditation and holistic form of healing. Yoga roughly translates to mean union of the mind and body. Most people associates yoga with the asanas or pose, but there are actually eight limbs of yoga that cultivates a true practice.

yama observances towards others
niyama self imposes disciplines
asana posture
pranayama control of life force (or breath as a rough translation)
pratyahara withdrawal of the senses
dharana concentration
dhyana meditative absorption
samadhi ecstasy
