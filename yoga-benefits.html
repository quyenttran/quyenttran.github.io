The Benefits of Yoga

Practicing yoga is proven to be beneficial to the body and mind. Here are some of the well known benefits:

  • Increased flexibility
  • Increased muscle strength and tone
  • Maintaining a balanced metabolism
  • Improved respiration, energy, and vitality
  • Increase blood flow
  • Helps with focus

These are just a few to name. For more details, checkout Yoga Journal's 38 Health Benefits of Yoga.
